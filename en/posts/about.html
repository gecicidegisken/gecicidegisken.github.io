What is "Temp Variable"?

Let me explain what's happening on this blog.

Hello world!

Temp Variable is a blog developed by a computer engineering undergrad student. It has named after the temporary variables which we use even the simplest algorithms.

Before I got started to university, I researched a lot about what do computer engineers do, which courses they take in school, what do they learn and similar questions. The whole content of this blog is the answers I found to these questions.
An archive to keep the projects I developed and the subjects & technologies I've learned.

I build the site simply with HTML&CSS. Using Github Pages for hosting (because it's free). Everything I wrote (codes, articles and design) is avaliable on my Github account , and there is a post about the technical details.

I hope to create a little archive in a while. I hope I can accomplish a good work to help both to myself and the people who asks the questions up there.

Since I'm from Turkey, Turkish students may be more relative with my university-related content. However I will try to write the English content global as much as possible. So, here in the English version there will be more self-learn content.

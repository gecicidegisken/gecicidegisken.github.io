Hello world!

Temp Variable is a blog developed by a computer engineering undergrad student. It has named after the temporary variables which we use simple swap algorithms.

Before I got started to school, I researched a lot about what do computer engineers do, which courses they take in school, what do they learn and similar questions.The whole content of this blog is the answers I found to these questions.
An archive to keep the projects I developed and the lectures & technologies I've learned. Not a source to learn but it tells what to learn and how to learn.

I build the site just simply using HTML&CSS. Used Github Pages a host (because it's free) and lastly I got my domain address.There is a post about technical details.

My plans is to create an archive in a while. I hope I can accomplish a good work to help both to myself and the people who asks the questions up there.
